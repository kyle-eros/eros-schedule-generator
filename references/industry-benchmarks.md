# OnlyFans Industry Benchmarks 2025

Current statistics, KPIs, and benchmarks for performance evaluation.

## Platform Statistics (2024-2025)

### OnlyFans Scale
- Gross revenue: $7.22 billion (2024)
- Creator payouts: $5.8 billion
- Platform commission: 20% flat
- Registered users: 377+ million fans
- Active creators: 4.6+ million

### Creator Economics
- Median monthly earnings: $145-180
- Average monthly earnings: $150-300
- Top 1% earn: 33% of total GMV
- Top 10% earn: 73% of total GMV
- Creators earning $100K+/year: ~4%

### User Behavior
- Average spend per paying user: $48.52
- Subscribers who make purchases: 4.2%
- Average fan lifespan: 44.85 days
- Conversation rate: 17.19% start DMs
- First purchase conversion: 3.27%
- Subscription renewal rate (paid): 18.4%
