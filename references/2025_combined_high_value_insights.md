## 1. Core Platform Truths

These fundamental realities apply to both paid and free page models:

| Truth | Implication |
|-------|-------------|
| 80%+ revenue from DMs | Feed content is marketing, not revenue |
| Paid pages cannot lock feed content | All wall posts accessible to subscribers |
| PPV sales happen EXCLUSIVELY in DMs | Never post PPV to the feed |
| Only 4.2% of subscribers purchase beyond subscription | Focus effort on converting this minority |

**Key Insight:** The feed exists to attract and retain subscribers. Revenue generation happens through direct message engagement and PPV strategy.

## 7. Welcome Message Strategy

### Paid Pages

| Element | Include | Exclude |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Warm personal greeting | Yes | Generic templates |
| Brief intro of content style | Yes | Long paragraphs |
| Clear posting schedule | Yes | Vague promises |
| Exclusive welcome gift | Yes (FREE content) | PPV offers |
| Sales pitch | No | Aggressive monetization |

**Paid Page Welcome Flow:**
1. Personal greeting with subscriber name
2. Express gratitude for subscription
3. Brief preview of what to expect
4. FREE exclusive content as gift
5. Soft invitation to engage

### Free Pages

| Element | Include | Why |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Immediate free value | Yes | Establish goodwill |
| Low-priced welcome PPV | Yes ($5-10) | Establish purchase behavior early |
| Personal touch | Yes | Build connection |
| Menu/offerings overview | Optional | Set expectations |

**Free Page Welcome Flow:**
1. Personal greeting
2. Free content delivery
3. Low-barrier welcome offer ($5-10 PPV)
4. Invitation to explore content

## 8. Follow-Up Sequences

### PPV Follow-Up Timing

| Timing | Message Type | Example Copy |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| 1 hour after PPV | Playful reminder | "Some of you didn't open my last message. I'm not mad... just shocked" |
| Next day | Final nudge | "Not sending this again. But if you missed it - check your inbox" |
| 2-4 days later | Unsend/archive | Remove to refresh and avoid spam perception |

### Follow-Up Best Practices

| Practice | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Maximum 2 follow-ups per PPV | Avoid spam perception |
| Vary tone between follow-ups | Maintain authenticity |
| Include urgency element | "Won't send again" creates FOMO |
| Archive after 2-4 days | Clean inbox, enables re-send later |
| Never follow-up whale exclusions | Protect VIP relationships |

### Bump Message Timing

| Timing After PPV | Purpose | Conversion Boost |
|------------------|---------|------------------|
| 15-30 minutes | Quick reminder | 10-15% additional |
| 30-45 minutes | Urgency push | 8-12% additional |
| 1 hour | Final same-day push | 5-8% additional |

## 11. Fan Segmentation

### Tier Definitions and Strategy

| Tier | % of Fans | Monthly Spend | Strategy |
|------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Whales | 0.01-1% | $200+/day | Dedicated personal attention |
| Solid Spenders | 5-15% | $50-200/month | Premium PPV, 2-3x weekly personal touches |
| Occasional | 10-20% | 1-3 purchases | Mass PPV $15-25, FOMO tactics |
| Lurkers | 75-85% | Subscription only | Minimal attention, $5-10 test PPV |

### Whale Identification Signals

| Signal | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Tips without being asked | Very High |
| Purchases within 2 hours of PPV | High |
| Asks about custom content | High |
| Multi-month subscriber | Medium-High |
| Consistent engagement | Medium |
| Responds to every message | Medium |

### Tier-Specific Tactics

| Tier | Do | Don't |
|------|----|----|
| Whales | Personal messages, exclusive offers, skip mass PPV | Include in mass blasts, ignore messages |
| Solid Spenders | Regular check-ins, early access | Treat as mass audience |
| Occasional | FOMO messaging, bundle deals | Over-personalize (time inefficient) |
| Lurkers | Low-price tests, minimal effort | Invest significant time |
