02

12-Step Pipeline

Step 1

Analyze

Load creator profile from database including page type, fan count, and historical performance metrics.

Query: get_creator_profile.sql
Step 2

Match Content

Filter captions by vault availability and freshness score. Only captions with freshness >= 30 qualify.

Threshold: freshness >= 30
Step 3

Match Persona

Apply voice boost multiplier based on creator persona matching. Boost range: 1.0x to 1.4x.

Boost: 1.0x - 1.4x multiplier
Step 4

Build Structure

Create 7-day time slot matrix based on volume level (Low/Mid/High/Ultra) and optimal posting hours.

Slots: 2-6 PPV per day
Step 4B

Quality Scoring

LLM-enhanced caption quality assessment using 4-factor scoring: Authenticity (35%), Hook (25%), CTA (20%), Conversion (20%).

Mode: LLM-only (Full mode)
Step 5

Assign Captions

Use Vose Alias weighted random selection algorithm to assign captions to time slots based on performance scores.

Algorithm: Vose Alias O(1)
Step 6

Generate Follow-ups

Create bump messages scheduled 15-45 minutes after high-performing PPV drops to maximize conversions.

Delay: 15-45 min after PPV
Step 7

Apply Drip Windows

Mark no-PPV zones during drip feed periods (typically 14:00-22:00) when enabled for the creator.

Window: 14:00-22:00 EST
Step 7B

Caption Enhancement

LLM-enhanced authenticity tweaks for persona matching. Maximum 15% length change to preserve original voice.

Mode: LLM-only (Full mode)
Step 8

Apply Page Type

Adjust pricing based on page type: paid pages get +10% pricing, free pages get -10% for competitive positioning.

Paid: +10% | Free: -10%
Step 9

Validate

Final validation check: spacing >= 3 hours, freshness compliance, content rotation, persona boost rates.

Pass rate target: 100%
Step 10

Return

Package schedule with metadata including generation stats, validation results, and performance estimates.

Output: ScheduleResult object
03

Algorithms

Vose Alias Method

O(n) setup, O(1) selection

Weighted random selection algorithm for caption assignment. Ensures high-performing captions are selected proportionally more often while maintaining variety.

Freshness Decay

F(t) = 100 * e^(-0.015 * days)

Exponential decay function preventing caption overuse. Captions below freshness score of 30 are excluded from selection pool.

Persona Boost

boost = 1.0 + (match_score * 0.4)

Voice matching multiplier ranging from 1.0x to 1.4x. Higher persona alignment with creator's tone increases conversion probability by 20-40%.

Quality Scoring

weight = (perf*0.4 + fresh*0.2 + qual*0.4) * persona * quality_mod

LLM-enhanced 4-factor quality assessment for caption ranking.

Authenticity 35%
Hook 25%
CTA 20%
Conversion 20%
